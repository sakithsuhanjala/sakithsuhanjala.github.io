University of Ruhuna

The University of Ruhuna is one of the leading universities in Sri Lanka. The University of Ruhuna was established by a special presidential Decree on 1st September 1978 as Ruhuna University college fulfilling a long cherished desire of the people of Southern Sri Lanka.

Today the University of Ruhuna has a proud history of about 40 years and the University consists of 10 faculties and 57 departments.
